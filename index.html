The Documentary

synopsis 

This documentary showcases the technology, teamwork, and people working behind the scenes to keep Western Australia safe.

Host Hammad Chaudhry is granted rare access inside WA Police, embarking on an unprecedented journey through the specialist units, cutting-edge technology, and high-pressure operations that protect the community every day.

From tactical response teams and police aviation to advanced drone systems and frontline officers, this is an unfiltered look at the people, decisions, and innovations operating behind the pulse of Perth and across one of the world's largest policing jurisdictions.
